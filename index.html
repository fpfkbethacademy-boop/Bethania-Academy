Our Rich History

FPFK Bethania Academy was established in 1992, originating from the vision of the Free Pentecostal Fellowship in Kenya (FPFK) Likoni Church Women’s Fellowship. Starting with an initial enrollment of just nine (9) learners and four (4) staff members, the institution was founded to meet the holistic needs of the community.

Under the leadership of Rev. Michael Onyango and with support from Swedish missionaries Mr. Stig Jakobson and Mr. & Mrs. Arne Frodin, the school grew progressively from Nursery to Primary and now Junior Secondary levels. Today, it stands as a pillar of excellence in the Likoni-Ukunda road area.
