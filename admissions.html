The Enrollment Process

  1. Pick or download the Admission Form.
  2. Submit the completed form with required documents.
  3. Learner undergoes a placement interview.
  4. Payment of admission and tuition fees.
  5. Official issuance of school uniform and booklist.

Admission Requirements

  • Original Birth Certificate & 2 Copies.
  • NEMIS Number / UPI Number.
  • Assessment reports from previous school.
  • 3 Recent Passport-size photographs.
  • Fully filled Medical Examination form.

Fee Payment Information

For security and transparency, all school fees should be paid through the following methods:

Bank Deposit M-Pesa Paybill Banker's Cheque

*Cash payments are not accepted at the school office.
