Support for Needy Learners

We are proud to support approximately 10% of our enrollment through various partnerships to ensure no child is left behind.

Aga Khan Foundation

In 2024, 44 students were supported with fees. In 2025, this support continued for learners across all grades, including our Junior Secondary School.

Local FPFK Church

The church currently supports 53 students in 2025 with both school fees and food programs, up from 44 students in 2024.
